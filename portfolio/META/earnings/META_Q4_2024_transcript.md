# Meta Q4 2024 Earnings Call Transcript
**Date:** January 29, 2025

## Key Highlights

- **Revenue:** $48.4 billion, up 21% YoY
- **Operating Income:** $23.4 billion
- **Operating Margin:** 48%
- **Family DAP:** 3.3 billion
- **Meta AI MAUs:** 700 million+

## Strategic Themes - "Defining Year"

### Five Major AI Opportunities
1. **Improved Advertising** - AI redefining ads into agent that delivers measurable results
2. **More Engaging Experiences** - Better recommendations + new AI content types
3. **Business Messaging** - Every business to have AI agent for support/sales
4. **Meta AI** - Target 1B+ users as leading personal AI assistant
5. **AI Devices** - Glasses as ideal form factor for AI

### Key Product Updates
- Ray-Ban Meta glasses a "real hit" - trajectory year for category
- Threads: 320M+ MAUs, adding 1M+ signups/day
- WhatsApp: 100M+ MAUs in US
- Reels reshared 4.5B times/day

### Infrastructure Investment
- Planning ~1GW capacity in 2025
- Building 2GW+ AI datacenter ("significant part of Manhattan")
- "Hundreds of billions" in AI infrastructure over long term
- Custom MTIA silicon deployed for ranking/recommendation inference
- Server useful lives extended to ~5.5 years

### Llama 4
- Training in progress
- Natively multimodal (omni-model)
- Agentic capabilities
- Goal: Lead (vs Llama 3 goal of being competitive)

## Guidance
- **Q1 2025 Revenue:** $39.5-41.8B (8-15% growth)
- **2025 Expenses:** $114-119B
- **2025 CapEx:** $60-65B

## Key Quotes

**Mark Zuckerberg:**
> "I expect this is going to be the year when a highly intelligent and personalized AI assistant reaches more than 1 billion people, and I expect Meta AI to be that leading AI assistant."

> "I think this will very well be the year when Llama and open source become the most advanced and widely used AI models as well."

**On AI Engineer Agent:**
> "2025 will be the year when it becomes possible to build an AI engineering agent that has coding and problem-solving abilities of around a good mid-level engineer."

**On Open Source:**
> "I think for our kind of national advantage, it's important that [the global open source standard] is an American standard."
