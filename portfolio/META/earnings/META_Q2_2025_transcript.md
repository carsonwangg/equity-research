# Meta Q2 2025 Earnings Call Transcript
**Date:** July 30, 2025

## Key Highlights

- **Revenue:** $47.5 billion, up 22% YoY
- **Operating Income:** $20.4 billion
- **Operating Margin:** 43%
- **Family DAP:** 3.4 billion
- **Meta AI MAUs:** 1 billion+

## Superintelligence Vision

### Meta Superintelligence Labs Established
- **Leadership:**
  - Alexandr Wang - overall lead
  - Nat Friedman - AI products and applied research
  - Shengjia Zhao - Chief Scientist
- **Infrastructure:**
  - Prometheus cluster: 1GW+ (world's first), coming 2026
  - Hyperion: Can scale to 5GW over several years
  - Multiple "titan clusters" in development

### Model Development
- Llama 4.1 and 4.2 in progress
- Next-gen models pushing frontier in next year
- "AI systems improving themselves" - slow but "undeniable"
- Definition: "AI that surpasses human intelligence in every way"

## Business Performance

### Advertising
- New AI recommendation model: +5% Instagram conversions, +3% Facebook
- Meaningful % of ad revenue from campaigns using genAI features
- End-to-end automated tools: $60B annual run rate

### Engagement
- Facebook: +5% time spent (AI recommendations)
- Instagram: +6% time spent
- Video time on Instagram up 20%+ YoY globally
- Video time on Facebook up 20%+ YoY in US

### AI Devices
- Ray-Ban Meta sales accelerating
- New Oakley Meta HSTNs launched
- Higher % using Meta AI, retention increasing
- Quest ecosystem engagement growing

## Strategic Reorganization
- Business units reorganized around key market segments
- Shift more resources to AI-focused opportunities

## Guidance & Investment
- **Q3 Revenue:** $47.5-50.5B
- **2025 Expenses:** $114-118B
- **2025 CapEx:** $66-72B
- **2026 Outlook:** 
  - CapEx growth "notably larger" than 2025
  - Expenses grow at "significantly faster rate"
  - Infrastructure costs largest driver

## Key Quotes

**Mark on Superintelligence:**
> "Developing superintelligence -- which we define as AI that surpasses human intelligence in every way -- we think is now in sight."

> "Take Superintelligence seriously... this is going to really shape all of our systems sooner rather than later."

**On Open Source:**
> "We will continue open sourcing some models... but as you approach real superintelligence, there is a whole different set of safety concerns."

**Susan on ROI:**
> "On the core AI side, we continue to see strong ROI... On the GenAI side, we are clearly much, much earlier on the return curve."
