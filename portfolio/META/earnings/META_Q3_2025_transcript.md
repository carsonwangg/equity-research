# Meta Q3 2025 Earnings Call Transcript
**Date:** October 29, 2025

## Key Highlights

- **Revenue:** $51.2 billion, up 26% YoY
- **Operating Income:** $20.5 billion
- **Operating Margin:** 40%
- **Family DAP:** 3.5 billion
- **Instagram MAUs:** 3 billion (milestone)
- **Threads DAUs:** 150 million+
- **Reels Annual Run Rate:** $50 billion+

## Meta Superintelligence Labs Update

### Progress
- "Highest talent density lab in the industry"
- Three key areas: Research (Shengjia Zhao/Rob Fergus), Product (Nat Friedman), Infrastructure (Aparna Ramani)
- Headcount adjustments made after initial buildout
- Front-loading compute capacity for various superintelligence timelines

### AI Strategy
- Vision: "Personal superintelligence for everyone"
- Open source approach: "When Meta innovates, everyone benefits"
- Three giant transformers run Facebook, Instagram, and ads recommendations
- Working on combining into single unified AI system

## Business Performance

### Engagement & Video
- Facebook time spent: +5% in Q3
- Threads: +10% time spent
- Instagram video: +30% YoY
- Vibes (AI creation tools) launched - retention good, usage growing

### Advertising
- Ad impressions: +14% YoY
- Average price per ad: +10% YoY
- Lattice architecture rolled out to app ads: +3% conversions
- New runtime ads ranking model: +2% lift in Instagram conversions
- Andromeda improvements: +14% ads quality on Facebook

### Advantage+ Momentum
- Streamlined creation flow completed for Sales, App, Lead campaigns
- $60B annual run rate through end-to-end automated solutions
- Video generation features adoption up 20% QoQ

### Business Messaging
- Click-to-WhatsApp ads: +60% YoY revenue
- Business AIs expanded to all eligible businesses in Mexico/Philippines
- US merchants can add Business AIs to websites

## AI Devices
- Ray-Ban Meta and Oakley Meta Vanguards selling well
- Meta Ray-Ban Display glasses sold out in 48 hours
- Demo slots booked through end of next month

## Guidance
- **Q4 Revenue:** $56-59B
- **2025 Expenses:** $116-118B (raised)
- **2025 CapEx:** $70-72B (raised)
- **2026 Outlook:**
  - CapEx growth "notably larger" than 2025
  - Expense growth "significantly faster" than 2025
  - MSL AI needs growing most

## Risks Flagged
- EU regulatory changes could impact European revenue "as early as this quarter"
- US youth-related trials scheduled for 2026 "may result in material loss"

## Key Quotes

**Mark on Superintelligence timing:**
> "Some people think that we'll get there in a few years, others think it'll be 5, 7 years, or longer. I think it's the right strategy to aggressively front-load building capacity."

**On Meta AI monetization:**
> "We don't need to succeed in all of these areas to have a good ROI. But if we do, then I think that we will be wildly happy with the investments that we're making."

**On AI Devices:**
> "AI glasses are going to be the main way that we integrate superintelligence into our day-to-day lives."
