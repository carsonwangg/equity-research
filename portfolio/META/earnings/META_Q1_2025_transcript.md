# Meta Q1 2025 Earnings Call Transcript  
**Date:** April 30, 2025

## Key Highlights

- **Revenue:** $42.3 billion, up 16% YoY (19% constant currency)
- **Operating Income:** $17.6 billion
- **Operating Margin:** 41%
- **Family DAP:** 3.4 billion
- **Meta AI MAUs:** ~1 billion

## Five Strategic AI Opportunities (Detailed)

### 1. Improved Advertising
- New ads recommendation model for Reels: +5% conversion rates
- 30% more advertisers using AI creative tools QoQ
- Goal: Businesses tell objective + budget, AI does the rest
- "AI will make advertising a meaningfully larger share of global GDP"

### 2. More Engaging Experiences
- 6-month improvements: Facebook +7%, Instagram +6%, Threads +35% time spent
- Threads: 350M+ MAUs, on track to become next major social app
- Video era continues, but "interactive content you can interact with" coming

### 3. Business Messaging
- WhatsApp: 3B+ MAUs, 100M+ in US
- Messenger: 1B+ MAUs
- Instagram DMs = Messenger volume
- Thailand/Vietnam in top 10/11 by revenue (vs 30s in GDP) due to messaging commerce

### 4. Meta AI
- ~1B monthly actives
- Focus: Personalization, voice conversations, entertainment
- New standalone Meta AI app launched
- Monetization playbook: Scale first, then revenue

### 5. AI Devices
- Ray-Ban Meta glasses: Sales tripled YoY, 4x MAUs
- New Oakley Meta HSTNs (performance glasses)
- Quest 3S making VR more accessible
- "AI glasses ideal form factor for AI and metaverse"

## Technology Updates
- Llama 4: "Most intelligent, best multimodal, lowest latency, most efficient models"
- Llama 4 Behemoth model coming
- GEM (Generative Ads Recommendation model): +5% conversions on Facebook Reels
- Advantage+ shopping: $20B+ annual run rate, +70% YoY
- 4M+ advertisers using genAI creative tools

## Guidance
- **Q2 Revenue:** $42.5-45.5B
- **2025 Expenses:** $113-118B (lowered from $114-119B)
- **2025 CapEx:** $64-72B (increased from $60-65B)

## Key Quotes

**Mark on AI timing:**
> "At each step along the way so far, we've observed the more aggressive assumptions or the fastest assumptions have been the ones that have most accurately predicted what would happen."

**On Meta AI:**
> "We're all going to have an AI that we talk to throughout the day -- while we're browsing content on our phones, and eventually as we're going through our days with glasses."

**Susan on EU regulatory risk:**
> "We cannot rule out that [the Commission] may seek to impose further modifications... that could have a significant negative impact on our European revenue, as early as the third quarter of 2025."
