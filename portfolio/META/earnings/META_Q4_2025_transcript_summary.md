# Meta Q4 2025 Earnings Call Transcript Summary
**Date:** January 28, 2026

## Key Financial Results

**Q4 2025:**
- Revenue: $58.9 billion, up 25% YoY
- Family of Apps Ad Revenue: $58.1 billion, up 24%
- Ad Impressions: Rose 18%
- Average Price per Ad: Increased 6% YoY
- Reality Labs Revenue: $955 million, down 12% (lapping Quest 3s launch)
- Daily Active People: Exceeded 3.5 billion in December
- Free Cash Flow: $14.1 billion
- Cash & Marketable Securities: $81.6 billion
- Employee Headcount: 78,800+, up 6%
- Capital Expenditures: $22.1 billion

## CEO Mark Zuckerberg Key Themes

### AI Acceleration
> "We are now seeing a major AI acceleration. I expect 2026 to be a year where this wave accelerates even further on several fronts. We're starting to see agents really work."

### Personal Superintelligence Vision
> "Our vision is building personal superintelligence. We're starting to see the promise of AI that understands our personal context, including our history, our interests, our content, and our relationships."

### MetaCompute & Infrastructure
> "I recently announced MetaCompute with the belief that being the most efficient at how we engineer, invest, and partner to build our infrastructure will become a strategic advantage."

### Reality Labs Trajectory
> "I expect Reality Labs losses this year to be similar to last year, and this will likely be the peak as we start to gradually reduce our losses going forward while continuing to execute on our vision."

## Q1 2026 Guidance
- Revenue: $53.5B - $56.5B range
- Foreign currency: ~4% tailwind to YoY growth

## Full Year 2026 Outlook
- Total Expenses: $162B - $169B
- Capital Expenditures: $115B - $135B (supporting Meta Superintelligence Labs)
- Operating Income: Expected above 2025 levels
- Tax Rate: 13% - 16% range

## Key Strategic Initiatives

### Product Engagement Improvements
- Facebook feed/video post views: +7% from optimizations
- Instagram original content: 75% of US recommendations in Q4 (up 10 percentage points)
- Threads time spent: +20%
- Meta AI media generation daily actives: tripled YoY

### Business Messaging
- WhatsApp paid messaging: exceeded $2B annual run rate in Q4
- US click-to-message ads revenue: +50% YoY

### AI Productivity Gains
- Output per engineer: +30% since start of 2025
- Power users of AI coding tools: output up 80% YoY

## Risk Factors Highlighted
- Legal and regulatory headwinds in EU and US
- Youth-related scrutiny and scheduled trials in US
- Reality Labs operating losses expected similar to 2025
- Diminishing currency tailwinds later in 2026
- EU less personalized ads offering changes

*Source: Motley Fool Earnings Call Transcript, January 28, 2026*
