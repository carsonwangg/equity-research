## 10-K FY2024 Key Highlights

### Business Overview
- **Mission:** Build the future of human connection and the technology that makes it possible
- **Segments:** Family of Apps (FoA) and Reality Labs (RL)
- **Revenue Source:** ~98% from advertising on Facebook and Instagram

### 2024 Investment Priorities
1. Generative AI across products
2. Discovery engine optimization
3. Metaverse and wearables
4. Threads growth
5. Monetization improvements
6. Platform integrity
7. Infrastructure capacity

### Cost Structure (FY 2024)
- **FoA:** 79% of total costs ($75.25B)
- **Reality Labs:** 21% of total costs ($19.88B)

### Strategic Initiatives
- **AI:** Meta AI assistant across apps, Ray-Ban Meta glasses
- **Wearables:** Orion AR prototype unveiled
- **Open Source:** Llama models available for developers
- **Metaverse:** Meta Quest devices, Meta Horizon platform

### Key Risk Factors
- User growth and engagement competition (especially TikTok)
- Apple iOS privacy changes impact on ad targeting
- Regulatory pressure (DMA, GDPR, FTC consent order)
- Reality Labs ongoing losses
- AI development and deployment risks

### Key Developments & Legal Matters

#### EU/Regulatory
- DMA "subscription for no ads" investigation - €200M fine issued April 2025; appealed
- Less Personalized Ads (LPA) launched November 2024 in Europe
- GDPR €1.2B fine (May 2023) under appeal with interim stay

#### FTC
- Modified consent order (2020) - FTC seeking substantial changes
- District Court appeal reversed on procedural grounds (May 2025)
- Matter stayed pending further resolution

#### Competition
- FTC antitrust trial concluded May 2025; decision pending Q4 2025 or later
- EC Facebook Marketplace fine €798M (Nov 2024); under appeal

#### Youth Safety
- Multiple lawsuits alleging "social media addiction" harm to minors
- First trial (Flo Health pixel case) resulted in plaintiff verdict on liability
- Statutory damages at $5,000 per class member (~1.6M class members claimed)

### Guidance/Commentary
- CapEx guidance: $70-72B for FY 2025
- Reality Labs expected to continue operating at a loss
- AI investments driving revenue growth but also cost increases
