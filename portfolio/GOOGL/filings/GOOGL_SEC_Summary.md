## 10-K FY2024 Key Highlights

### Financial Performance
- **Total Revenue:** $350+ billion (12% YoY growth)
- **Operating Income:** Record high with 32% operating margin in Q4
- **Capital Expenditures:** Significant investment in technical infrastructure, particularly for AI

### Business Segments
| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Google Services** | Search, YouTube, Android, Chrome, devices, Gmail, Maps, Play |
| **Google Cloud** | GCP, Google Workspace, enterprise AI solutions |
| **Other Bets** | Waymo, life sciences, other moonshots |

### Strategic Priorities
1. **AI Leadership** - Gemini 2.0 model rollout across all products
2. **Cloud Growth** - AI-optimized infrastructure and enterprise solutions  
3. **Advertising Innovation** - AI-powered ad targeting and measurement
4. **Moonshots** - Waymo autonomous driving commercialization

### Risk Factors Highlighted
- Antitrust litigation (DOJ Search case ruling against Google in Aug 2024)
- Advertising technology litigation
- Privacy and data protection regulations (GDPR, DMA, DSA)
- AI development risks and competition
- International operations and currency exposure
