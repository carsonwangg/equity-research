# Alphabet Q4 2024 Earnings Call Transcript
**Date:** Q4 2024

## Key Highlights

- **Revenue:** $96.5 billion, up 12% YoY
- **Operating Income:** $31 billion, up 31%
- **Operating Margin:** 32%
- **Cloud Revenue:** $12 billion, up 30%
- **YouTube + Cloud combined run rate:** $110 billion

## Strategic Highlights

### AI Infrastructure
- Broke ground on 11 new Cloud regions and data center campuses
- 7 new subsea cable projects announced
- Google data centers deliver ~4x more computing power per unit of electricity vs 5 years ago
- Cloud customers consume 8x more compute for AI vs 18 months ago

### Model Development
- Gemini 2.0 launched (most capable model yet)
- Gemini 2.0 Flash and Flash Thinking models
- Deep Research launched in Gemini Advanced
- Project Mariner and Project Astra in trusted tester access
- Veo 2 (video) and Imagen 3 (image) models
- 4.4 million developers using Gemini models (2x from 6 months ago)

### Search & AI Overviews
- AI Overviews in 100+ countries
- Circle to Search on 200M+ Android devices
- Users who tried Circle to Search use it for 10%+ of searches
- "2025 going to be one of biggest years for Search innovation yet"

### Cloud Wins
- Mercedes-Benz, Mercado Libre, Servier as new customers
- First-time commitments doubled vs 2023
- Multiple deals over $1 billion
- Deals over $250 million doubled vs prior year
- Trillium (6th gen TPU): 4x better training, 3x inference throughput
- Vertex AI 5x customer increase, 20x usage increase in 2024

### YouTube
- #1 in streaming watchtime in US (record high share)
- 250,000+ creators in Shopping affiliate program (US/Korea)
- Shorts monetization rate increased 30+ percentage points vs in-stream (US)
- Shorts on CTV now 15% of Shorts viewing (US)
- Most popular service for podcast listening (Edison)

### Waymo
- 4 million passenger trips in 2024
- 150,000+ trips/week
- Expanding to Austin, Atlanta (2025), Miami (2026)
- Tokyo testing announced
- 6th gen Waymo driver in development (lower hardware costs)

## 2025 Guidance
- **CapEx:** ~$75 billion
- First quarter impacted by FX headwinds and leap year comparison
- Depreciation growth to accelerate
- Cloud revenue may have variability based on capacity deployment timing

## Key Quotes

**Sundar Pichai on Search:**
> "AI is driving an expansionary moment for Search. As people learn what they can do with our new AI experiences, they are increasingly coming back to search more."

**On DeepSeek:**
> "For us, it's always been obvious over time... you can drive a lot of efficiency to serve these models really well... I think we lead this Pareto frontier."

**Philipp on AI Overviews monetization:**
> "For AI Overviews overall, we actually see monetization at approximately the same rate."
