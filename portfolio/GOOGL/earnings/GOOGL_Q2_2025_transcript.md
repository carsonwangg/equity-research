# Alphabet (GOOGL) Q2 2025 Earnings Call Summary
**Date:** July 23, 2025

## Key Participants
- **Sundar Pichai** - CEO
- **Ruth Porat** - President and Chief Investment Officer
- **Philipp Schindler** - SVP and Chief Business Officer

---

## Financial Highlights

| Metric | Q2 2025 | YoY Change |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Total Revenue | $96.4B | +14% (beat $94B estimate) |
| Diluted EPS | $2.31 | +22% (beat $2.18 estimate) |
| Operating Expenses | $26.1B | +20% |
| Google Search & Other | $54.1B | Double-digit growth |
| Google Cloud | $13.6B | +32% (beat ~27% estimate) |

---

## CEO Commentary (Sundar Pichai)

### AI Impact Across Business
> "AI is positively impacting all parts of the business, supported by investments in infrastructure, research, models, and product platforms."

### Search Evolution
> "AI Overviews now has over 2 billion monthly users across more than 200 countries and 40 languages."

> "AI Mode has launched in the U.S. and India, representing the next chapter of Search."

### YouTube Monetization
> "YouTube Shorts now earns as much revenue per watch hour as traditional in-stream video in the U.S., and exceeds in-stream rates in some countries."

---

## Business Segment Performance

### Google Search
- Revenue: $54.1B (slightly beat estimates)
- AI Overviews: 2B+ monthly users globally
- AI Mode expanding internationally
- Continued double-digit growth

### Google Cloud
- Revenue: $13.6B (+32% YoY)
- Significantly beat expectations (consensus ~27%)
- Annual run rate now exceeds $50B
- Strong AI product demand driving growth

### YouTube
- Strong momentum in both ads and subscriptions
- Shorts monetization reaching parity with traditional video
- International markets showing strength

---

## Operating Expenses
- Total: $26.1B (+20% YoY)
- Includes $1.4B legal settlement charge
- Higher R&D spending on AI initiatives

---

## Key Themes

1. **Cloud Outperformance** - 32% growth significantly beating expectations; AI demand accelerating
2. **Search Resilience** - AI Overviews at 2B users; AI Mode rolling out
3. **YouTube Shorts Monetization** - Parity with traditional video; international strength
4. **Full Stack AI Investment** - Infrastructure, models, and applications all growing
5. **Antitrust Overhang** - Earnings release ahead of antitrust ruling

---

## Context
- Earnings beat on both revenue and EPS
- Cloud growth accelerating beyond expectations
- AI integration driving engagement and monetization
- Legal costs impacting operating expenses
