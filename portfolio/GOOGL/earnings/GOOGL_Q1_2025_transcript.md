# Alphabet (GOOGL) Q1 2025 Earnings Call Summary
**Date:** April 24, 2025

## Key Participants
- **Sundar Pichai** - CEO
- **Ruth Porat** - President and Chief Investment Officer
- **Philipp Schindler** - SVP and Chief Business Officer

---

## Financial Highlights

| Metric | Q1 2025 | YoY Change |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Total Revenue | $90.2B | +12% (+14% constant currency) |
| Net Income | $34.5B | +46% |
| Diluted EPS | $2.81 | +49% |
| Operating Margin | 34% | +2 ppts |
| Google Search & Other | $50.7B | Double-digit growth |
| YouTube Ads | $8.9B | Double-digit growth |
| Google Cloud | $12.3B | +28% |
| Cloud Operating Income | $2.2B | Strong profitability |

---

## CEO Commentary (Sundar Pichai)

### AI Leadership
> "Gemini 2.5 is the most intelligent AI model we've built, achieving breakthroughs in performance and recognized as the best model in the industry."

### Search & AI Integration
> "AI Overviews achieved over 1.5 billion monthly users. We're seeing positive early reaction to AI Mode, which represents the next evolution of Search."

### Infrastructure Investment
> "Ironwood, our seventh-generation TPU, delivers 10X improvement in compute power while being nearly twice as power efficient."

> "We were the first cloud provider to offer NVIDIA's B200 and GB200 Blackwell GPUs."

### Subscriptions Growth
> "We surpassed 270 million paid subscriptions, with YouTube and Google One as key drivers."

---

## Business Segment Performance

### Google Search
- Revenue: $50.7B with continued double-digit growth
- AI Overviews: 1.5B+ monthly users
- AI Mode launched with positive early traction

### Google Cloud
- Revenue: $12.3B (+28% YoY)
- Growth driven by GCP core products, AI Infrastructure, and Generative AI Solutions
- Strong operating income of $2.2B

### YouTube
- Ads revenue: $8.9B with double-digit growth
- Subscriptions momentum continuing
- Strong engagement metrics

---

## Capital Returns
- Dividend increased 5% to $0.21 per share quarterly
- Continued share repurchase program

---

## Key Themes

1. **AI Integration Everywhere** - Gemini 2.5 powering products across Search, Cloud, and YouTube
2. **Search Transformation** - AI Overviews and AI Mode redefining search experience
3. **Cloud Momentum** - 28% growth with strong profitability and AI demand
4. **Infrastructure Leadership** - TPU v7 (Ironwood) and first-to-market with NVIDIA Blackwell
5. **Subscriptions Scale** - 270M+ paid subscribers across platforms
