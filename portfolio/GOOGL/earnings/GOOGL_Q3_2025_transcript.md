# Alphabet Q3 2025 Earnings Call Transcript
**Date:** October 29, 2025

## Key Highlights

- **Revenue:** $102.35 billion (first-ever $100B quarter), up 16% YoY
- **Operating Income:** $31.2 billion, up 9% (33.9% margin ex-EC fine)
- **Cloud Revenue:** $15.2 billion, up 34%
- **Cloud Backlog:** $155 billion, up 46% QoQ
- **Search Revenue:** $56.6 billion, up 15%
- **YouTube Ads:** $10.3 billion, up 15%

## Strategic Highlights

### AI & Models
- Gemini models process 7 billion tokens/minute via direct API
- Gemini App: 650M+ monthly active users, queries up 3x from Q2
- Processing 1.3 quadrillion monthly tokens (20x growth in a year)
- Gemini 3 coming later this year
- 7th gen TPU Ironwood coming GA soon
- Willow quantum chip achieved major breakthrough (13,000x faster than supercomputers)

### Search & AI Mode
- AI Overviews: 2B+ users across 200+ countries
- AI Mode: 75M+ daily active users, rolled out to 40 languages
- AI Mode driving incremental total query growth
- Over 100 improvements shipped to AI Mode in Q3
- Younger users particularly appreciate AI experiences

### Cloud Performance
- New GCP customers up 34% YoY
- More deals over $1B in first 9 months than previous 2 years combined
- 70%+ of existing customers use AI products
- 13 product lines each at $1B+ annual run rate
- Revenue from generative AI models grew 200%+ YoY

### YouTube
- #1 in streaming watch time for 2+ years (Nielsen)
- First live NFL broadcaster (19M viewers, record concurrent)
- Shorts earn more revenue/watch hour than in-stream in US

### Waymo
- Opening London, Tokyo next year
- Expanding to Dallas, Nashville, Denver, Seattle
- Autonomous at San Jose/San Francisco airports
- Waymo for Business launched
- Waymo Teens accounts launched in Phoenix

## Financial Guidance
- **2025 CapEx:** $91-93 billion (up from $85B estimate)
- **2026 CapEx:** Significant increase expected
- Q4 advertising comps impacted by 2024 election spending

## Key Quotes

**Sundar on AI Mode:**
> "AI Mode is resonating well with users. In the US, we've seen strong and consistent week-over-week growth in usage since launch, and queries doubled over the quarter."

**Anat on Cloud:**
> "We still expect to remain in a tight demand/supply environment in Q4 and 2026."

**On monetization:**
> "For AI Overviews, even at our current baseline of ads, below and within the AI response, overall, we see the monetization at approximately the same rate."
